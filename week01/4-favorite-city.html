Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu, located about 100 kilometers from Cusco, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and Peru's most famous archaeological site. Built by the Incas in the 15th century and rediscovered in 1911, it is known for its well-preserved ruins, breathtaking mountain setting, andremarkable structures such as the Temple of the Sun and the Intihuatana stone.
